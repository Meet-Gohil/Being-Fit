Tricep Exercises

Tricep Dips

Description:

Strengthens the triceps, chest, and shoulders. Builds upper body strength and muscle definition. Can be performed with body weight or added weight.

How to Perform:

  • 1.Use parallel bars or a dip station. Support your body weight with your arms.
  • 2.Lower your body by bending your elbows until your upper arms are parallel to the floor.
  • 3.Push yourself back up to the starting position.

    • Tricep Pushdowns

    Description:

    Isolates the triceps, focusing on the lateral head. Helps build tricep strength and definition.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand in front of a cable machine with a high pulley. Attach a bar or rope to the pulley.
  • 2.Grip the bar or rope with an overhand grip and pull it down to your thighs.
  • 3.Push the handle down by extending your elbows, keeping your upper arms stationary.
  • 4.Slowly return to the starting position.

    • Overhead Tricep Extension

    Description:

    Stretches and strengthens the long head of the triceps. Enhances muscle size and flexibility.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand or sit with a dumbbell or barbell held above your head with both hands.
  • 2. Keep your elbows close to your head and lower the weight behind your head by bending your elbows.
  • 3.Extend your elbows to return to the starting position.

    • Skull Crushers

    Description:

    Focuses on the triceps' long head. Enhances muscle strength and size.

    How to Perform:

  • 1. Lie flat on a bench with a barbell or dumbbells in hand, arms extended above your chest.
  • 2.Bend your elbows to lower the weight towards your forehead or just above your head.
  • 3.Extend your elbows to return to the starting position.

    • Close-Grip Bench Press

    Description:

    Targets the triceps while also working the chest and shoulders. Builds overall upper body strength and muscle mass.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Lie flat on a bench with your feet flat on the floor. Grasp the barbell with a narrow grip, hands closer than shoulder-width.
  • 2.Lower the barbell to your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body.
  • 3.Push the barbell back up to the starting position.

    • Dumbbell Kickbacks

    Description:

    Provides a full-body workout, burning calories and improving cardiovascular fitness. Strengthens the upper body, core, and legs.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Bend at the waist with a dumbbell in one hand, the other hand resting on a bench for support.
  • 2.Keep your upper arm parallel to the ground and extend your elbow to straighten your arm.
  • 3.Return to the starting position with a controlled motion.
  • 4.Repeat on the other arm.

    • Tricep Kickbacks

    Description:

    Isolates and strengthens the triceps, especially the lateral and long heads. Helps improve overall muscle tone.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand with feet hip-width apart and hold a dumbbell in one hand.
  • 2.Bend at the waist and keep your back straight. Extend your arm behind you, keeping your upper arm parallel to the floor.
  • 3.Return to the starting position, maintaining control throughout the movement.
