General Guidelines for Gaining Muscle Mass

  • Increase Caloric Intake: Consume more calories than you burn to support muscle growth.
  • Protein-Rich Foods: Incorporate a variety of plant-based proteins to meet your protein needs.
  • Complex Carbohydrates: Include whole grains, legumes, and starchy vegetables for energy.
  • Healthy Fats: Add nuts, seeds, avocados, and oils to boost caloric intake and support hormone production.
  • Frequent Meals: Eat every 3-4 hours to provide your body with a steady supply of nutrients.
