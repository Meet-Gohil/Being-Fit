Abs Exercises

Crunches

Description:

Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands behind your head without pulling on your neck. Lift your shoulders off the floor and curl your upper body towards your knees, then slowly lower back down.

Leg Raises

Description:

Lie flat on your back with your legs extended. Place your hands under your lower back for support. Lift your legs up to a 90-degree angle and then slowly lower them back down without touching the floor.

Russian Twists

Description:

Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet slightly off the ground. Lean back slightly and hold your hands together or a weight. Rotate your torso to the right and then to the left to complete one rep.

Mountain Climbers

Description:

Start in a plank position. Bring one knee towards your chest, then quickly switch legs, simulating a running motion while keeping your upper body stationary.

Flutter Kicks

Description:

Lie on your back with your hands under your buttocks for support. Lift your legs slightly off the ground and kick them up and down in a quick, scissor-like motion.

V-Ups

Description:

Lie on your back with your arms extended overhead and legs straight. Simultaneously lift your upper body and legs to form a V shape, reaching your hands towards your feet.

Bicycle Crunches

Description:

Lie on your back and lift your knees to a 90-degree angle. Place your hands behind your head and bring your opposite elbow towards your opposite knee while straightening the other leg. Alternate sides in a pedaling motion.
