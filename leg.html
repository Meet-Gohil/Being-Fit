Legs Exercises

Squats

Description:

Strengthens the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and core. Improves balance and flexibility.

How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointing slightly outward.
  • 2.Keep your chest up and back straight, and extend your arms in front of you or clasp them at your chest.
  • 3.Engage your core and begin to lower your body by bending your knees and hips, as if you're sitting back into a chair.
  • 4.Lower yourself until your thighs are at least parallel to the ground, keeping your weight on your heels.
  • 5.Push through your heels to return to the starting position

    • Lunges

    Description:

    Targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Enhances stability and coordination.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand with your feet hip-width apart, hands on your hips or by your sides.
  • 2.Step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle. Your back knee should hover just above the ground.
  • 3.Ensure your front knee is directly above your ankle and does not push past your toes.
  • 4.Push through your front heel to return to the starting position.
  • 5.Repeat with the other leg.

    • Leg Press

    Description:

    Works the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Allows for controlled movement with heavier weights.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Sit on a leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform.
  • 2.Engage your core and press the platform away from you by extending your knees and hips.
  • 3.Ensure your feet stay flat on the platform throughout the movement.
  • 4.Slowly return to the starting position by bending your knees and hips, keeping your back flat against the seat.

    • Calf Raises

    Description:

    Strengthens the calf muscles, improving ankle stability and overall leg strength.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand with your feet hip-width apart, hands on a wall or a sturdy surface for balance.
  • 2.Raise your heels off the ground as high as possible, balancing on the balls of your feet.
  • 3.Hold the top position for a moment, then slowly lower your heels back to the ground.

    • Deadlifts

    Description:

    Engages the hamstrings, glutes, lower back, and core. Enhances overall strength and functional fitness.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand with your feet hip-width apart, holding a barbell or dumbbells in front of your thighs with an overhand grip.
  • 2.Engage your core and keep your back straight as you hinge at the hips, lowering the weight towards the ground.
  • 3.Keep the weight close to your body, lowering it until it reaches mid-shin level.
  • 4.Push through your heels and extend your hips and knees to return to the starting position.

    • Step-Ups

    Description:

    Targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Improves balance and coordination.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Stand in front of a sturdy bench or step with your feet hip-width apart.
  • 2.Step onto the bench with one foot, pressing through your heel to lift your body up.
  • 3.Bring your other foot up to meet the first foot on the bench.
  • 4.Step back down with the same foot, followed by the other foot.
  • 5.Repeat on the other leg.

    • Glute Bridges

    Description:

    Strengthens the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back. Helps improve hip mobility and stability.

    How to Perform:

  • 1.Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart. Place your arms at your sides with palms facing down.
  • 2.Engage your core and squeeze your glutes to lift your hips off the ground until your body forms a straight line from your shoulders to your knees.
  • 3.Hold the top position for a moment, then slowly lower your hips back to the ground.
